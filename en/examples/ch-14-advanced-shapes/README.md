# Chapter 14 examples · Advanced Shapes

> Support label: **BASELINE** (mask, polygon, border-image, mix-blend-mode) · **BASELINE 2026** (shape()) · **BEHIND FLAG** (corner-shape)

## Shapes that used to need SVG, now in pure CSS

Three demos in one page, all BASELINE today:

- **Notched ticket**: side notches punched with `mask` + two radial gradients and `mask-composite: intersect`. No image.
- **mix-blend-mode**: overlapping circles tint where they meet with `multiply`.
- **border-image**: a gradient border instead of a flat color line.

**Open it:** `index.html` in this folder, or live at
https://alexanderuk82.github.io/modern-css-ebook/en/c14/shapes/

`shape()` (BASELINE 2026) and `corner-shape` squircles (BEHIND FLAG) are covered
in the chapter with their labels.
